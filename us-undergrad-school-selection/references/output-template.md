# Output Template

Use this structure for a full custom list unless the user requests a different format.

## 1. Student Snapshot
Summarize the student's profile, goals, budget, and major constraints in 4-8 bullets.

## 2. Assumptions and Caveats
List any assumptions you had to make and which ones most affect the list.

## 3. Strategy Summary
Explain in 1 short paragraph:
- how selective the overall list should be
- how finance changes the strategy
- whether the family should expect true safeties, financial anchors, or both

## 4. Recommended 20-School List
Provide a table with these columns:
- Tier
- School
- Why it fits
- Key sourced facts
- Cost or aid note
- Main risk

## 5. School-by-School Rationale
For each school, give 2-4 concise bullets covering:
- academic or major fit
- admissions evidence from CDS or official pages
- finance or scholarship angle
- key tradeoff or caution

## 6. Next-Step Advice
Close with practical guidance such as:
- which schools are best for ED or EA consideration
- where to do deeper department-level research
- which schools need finance verification before staying on the list

## Source Discipline
Include exact links in the final answer whenever possible. When multiple official sources are used for a school, prefer one CDS link plus one admissions or aid link.
